# Landing Page Override

> Overrides `design-system/officia/MASTER.md` for the marketing landing.

## Direction
Industrial ledger / gold-trust fintech. Dark slate atmosphere with amber as the brand signal — not purple SaaS glow.

## Hero (first viewport)
- Brand **Officia** is the dominant hero-level wordmark (larger than the headline)
- One headline, one short supporting sentence, one CTA group
- Full-bleed visual plane: warm amber light + ledger grid + product silhouette (edge-to-edge, not a card)
- No metrics strip, badges, pills, or overlays in the hero
- Motions: brand reveal, ambient light drift, CTA shine

## Colors
- Background: `#0B1220`
- Text: `#F8FAFC`
- Primary / CTA: `#F59E0B` → `#FBBF24`
- Muted: `#94A3B8`

## Typography
- Brand / headings: Fira Code
- Body: Fira Sans
